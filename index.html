About Me

I’m a 23-year-old woman, born and raised in Barcelona to Chinese immigrant parents. Currently in my final year of International Business Economics at Universitat Pompeu Fabra, I’ve grown up in a multicultural environment shaped by my family's small convenience store and a blend of Eastern and Western values.

As a Third Culture Kid, I’ve had the privilege of connecting with people from diverse backgrounds, which has enriched my worldview—though it has also made me reflect deeply on identity and belonging. These experiences have shaped my appreciation for diversity, empathy, and open-mindedness.

Outside of academics, I enjoy playing the piano, exploring photography, and discovering new places through travel. Recently, I’ve also embraced running and going to the gym as part of my personal growth. At my core, I stand for fairness, authenticity, and the celebration of different cultures.
